# WBSender – Streamlined WhatsApp Bulk Messaging for Businesses

**WBSender** is a powerful and intuitive WhatsApp bulk messaging platform designed to help businesses engage customers at scale.  
It offers smart contact management, campaign automation, and an elegant landing experience — making large-scale communication effortless and efficient.

## ✨ Features

- 💬 **Bulk Messaging** – Send personalized messages to hundreds of contacts in one click
- 📤 **Excel Contact Import** – Upload `.xlsx` files, scan them instantly, and get a summary of imported vs. duplicate contacts
- 🧮 **Smart Validation System** – Automatically detects and skips invalid or duplicate numbers during import
- 🧭 **Server-Based Filtering** – Dynamically search and filter contacts (e.g., by range 30–60) for campaign targeting
- 🧩 **Campaign Template System** – Reusable message templates to streamline repetitive campaigns
- ⚡ **Campaign Creation Flow** – Multi-step, server state-driven modal that visually tracks progress through 5 steps, handling successes and stops gracefully
- ⚙️ **Contact Management System** – Save imported contacts for future campaigns with easy access
- 🌐 **Modern Landing Page** – Built from scratch with V0 and custom enhancements for a sleek, professional agency presentation
